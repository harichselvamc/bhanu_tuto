Why RV Cricket Gears?

When it comes to choosing a name for a brand, the story behind it often holds a special place in the founder’s heart. For Mr. Rakesh Kumar R, the founder of RV Cricket Gears, this wasn’t just a business decision—it was a tribute to the very roots of his inspiration.

The name “RV” isn’t just an abbreviation; it carries the initials of his beloved parents, Ranjith and Vinitha. Growing up, Rakesh witnessed their unwavering support, love, and encouragement, which shaped his dreams and ambitions. It was their belief in him that gave him the confidence to start his own venture.

By embedding their names into his brand, Rakesh not only honors his parents but also carries their legacy forward with every cricket bat, ball, and gear that RV Cricket Gears delivers. It serves as a constant reminder that the foundation of this company is built on family values, integrity, and a passion to bring the best to cricket enthusiasts worldwide.

For Rakesh, every shipment sent out isn’t just a product sold—it’s a story of love, gratitude, and pride. And that’s what makes RV Cricket Gears more than just a brand—it makes it a family.
